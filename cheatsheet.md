## Cheat Sheet

### Markdown (.md)

```
# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4

*Italic Text*
**Bold Text**

> Blockquote

[Link Text](http://www.linkadress.com)

![Image Title](http://www.imagesource.com/assets/image.jpg)

[^1] Footnote number in text
[^1]: The footnote itself is the same thing, but with a colon followed by the footnote text

- dashes make
- a list with
- bullets

1. numbers make
2. a list with
3. numbers
```

### HTML

```
<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<h3>Heading 3</h3>
<h4>Heading 4</h4>

<em>Italic Text</em>
<strong>Bold Text</strong>

<blockquote>Blockquote</blockquote>

<a href="http://www.linkadress.com">Link Text</a>

<img src="http://www.imagesource.com/assets/image.jpg" alt="Image Title" />

<sup>1</sup> Footnote number in text

<ul>
  <li>A bulleted</li>
  <li>list</li>
  <li>of items</li>
</ul>

<ol>
  <li>A numbered</li>
  <li>list</li>
  <li>of items</li>
</ol>

<p>Paragraph of text</p>

<br /> Line break

```

### Special Characters

```
— | &#8212; | em dash
– | &#8211; | en dash
“ | &#8220; | left double quote
” | &#8221; | right double quote
‘ | &#8216; | left single quote
’ | &#8217; | right single quote
```
